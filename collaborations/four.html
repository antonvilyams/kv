USER EXPERIENCE DESIGN

Four x Kristina Vilyams

November 2020

Collaboration with Four to refine the user interface design for their mobile app and website, enhancing the user experience. The project also involved developing brand identity materials and creating digital assets and templates for marketing communications, strengthening the brand’s cohesion.

Four is a Miami-based fintech company specializing in flexible split-payment solutions. Since 2018, it has been transforming the Buy Now, Pay Later (BNPL) landscape with innovative, interest-free payment options that enhance both customer experience and retailer performance.
