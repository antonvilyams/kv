YOUR MISAMISA & DOLL ON A FARM

ThugPop x Kristina Vilyams

June 2021

Collaboration with Christen Mooney (also known as ThugPop) on the DOLL ON A FARM and YOUR MISAMISA AR try-on effects, translating his bold visual language into immersive digital experiences.

DOLL ON A FARM was featured in Lenslist’s Weekly Selection #54 of 2021.

Christen Mooney, also known as ThugPop, is a multidisciplinary artist and cultural figure whose fearless vision has made him a defining presence in contemporary fashion and art. Celebrated for collaborations with trailblazing designers such as Jean Paul Gaultier, Lotta Volkova, Shayne Oliver, and Telfar—and as a longtime muse for Hood By Air—Mooney has cemented his place at the intersection of art, fashion, and performance. His unapologetic aesthetic and uncompromising voice have also earned recognition in 10 Magazine and Interview Magazine, reinforcing his reputation as one of the boldest creatives shaping today’s cultural landscape.

Christen Mooney: Art as Defiance, Fashion as Future

After establishing a presence in New York’s fashion scene, Christen Mooney—better known as ThugPop—emerged as a multidisciplinary creative whose practice spans fashion, art, and performance. He garnered early critical attention with his tapestry series Cooning For Cash, inspired by the 1970s photography of Bob Mizer’s Athletic Model Guild. The series was widely noted for its exploration of identity, self-acceptance, and the layered dimensions of masculinity and representation.

Mooney’s artistry is both deeply visual and deeply personal—shaped by spiritual iconography, bold aesthetics, and a tactile language of glitter, bows, textiles, and collage informed by cultural memory and lived experience. His work resonates not only for its aesthetic rigor, but for its emotional depth and sincerity.

Since then, Mooney has become a defining voice in contemporary visual culture. He has collaborated with influential figures and labels including Jean Paul Gaultier, Lotta Volkova, Shayne Oliver, Telfar, and Anonymous Club, and has served as a longtime muse for Hood By Air. His influence extends beyond the visual arts—he is also recognized as a model, stylist, DJ, and musician—renowned for defying conventions and championing underrepresented narratives with confidence, flair, and unwavering creativity. With editorial features in 10 Magazine and Interview Magazine, Mooney has solidified his reputation as a visionary artist who continues to push boundaries across multiple mediums.

Glitter, Play, and Defiance: DOLL ON A FARM and YOUR MISAMISA

When Christen Mooney set out to expand his artistry into new digital dimensions, Kristina Vilyams translated his eclectic vision into augmented reality. Together, they created two signature AR try-on experiences, DOLL ON A FARM and YOUR MISAMISA. Each effect was crafted to channel Mooney’s unapologetically bold aesthetic, merging surreal imagery, playfulness, and identity-driven narratives into immersive digital spaces.

DOLL ON A FARM was spotlighted in Lenslist’s Weekly Selection #54 (2021), praised for its inventive use of extended reality and its alignment with Mooney’s genre-defying approach, while YOUR MISAMISA invited audiences into an equally vibrant, interactive encounter that amplified his artistic universe in new ways.
