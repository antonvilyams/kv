Art Direction for Fashion: Course Project

Photography by Jeff Brouws, Moni Haworth, Fashion Styling by Lotta Volkova, Model Amber Lucia for JennyFax Fall/Winter 2021

December 9, 2024

Sunset Grunge: California’s Shadowed Allure — a POP Magazine–style editorial project by Kristina Vilyams, developed as part of the Art Direction for Fashion course at Central Saint Martins, University of the Arts London. Created under the guidance of Schelay McCarter, Creative Director/Producer and Associate Lecturer at CSM, the project was later featured on Schelay’s Fashiontent blog.
